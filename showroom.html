About Showroom

Balaji Marble & Sanitary is a modern showroom offering a wide range of tiles, marble, granite, sanitary ware, faucets, and plumbing materials. Located at Dhara Nagar Gate, Mahavir Market, Abrama, Dharampur Road, Valsad. We help customers design beautiful kitchens, bathrooms, and interiors with premium materials.
